Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to all games, applications, and services ("apps") developed and published by Kynotes S, including those available on Meta platforms such as the Meta Quest Store and App Lab. It outlines what data we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and how you can manage your information.

2. Data We Collect

When you use our VR games or applications, we may collect the following data:

A. Hardware ID

A unique identifier for your device, used to manage logins and user accounts via third-party services like PlayFab.

B. IP Address

Collected and stored to support account security, moderation, and analytics.

C. Gameplay Data

We may collect in-game activity (such as selected options, scores, or session info) for gameplay functionality and analytics.

D. Platform Data

Meta platform data (e.g., Oculus ID or App ID) may be accessed in accordance with Meta's Data Use Policy to support your app experience.

3. Purpose of Data Collection

A. Account and Login Management

Used for identifying returning users, managing game progress, and syncing game data through services like PlayFab.

B. Game Functionality

Some data is essential for delivering features such as online multiplayer, voting, or matchmaking.

C. Moderation and Security

We use IP and user identifiers to detect and prevent misuse, enforce community standards, and manage bans or restrictions.

D. Analytics and Improvements

We analyze anonymized usage data to improve our apps and provide better user experiences.

E. External Services

We use third-party services to manage some data processing. Please refer to their policies:

PlayFab: Terms of Use
Photon: Privacy and Cookie Policy

4. Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your data or your account associated with any Kynotes app, please email us with your user ID or device ID. Upon verification, we will remove your data from our systems and any connected third-party services.

5. Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us

6. Updates

This policy may be updated as our apps evolve or as we integrate new services. We recommend reviewing this page
