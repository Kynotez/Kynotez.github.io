Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data we collect, the purposes for which we use it, how we manage and delete your data, and how you can contact us regarding privacy concerns.

2. Data We Collect

A. Hardware ID

We collect your Hardware ID, a unique identifier for your device or system, which is sent to PlayFab and used for logging into your account.

B. IP Address

We store your IP address in your PlayFab account.

3. Purpose of Data Collection

A. Account Login

Your Hardware ID and IP address are used to log you into your PlayFab account.

B. Moderation

Your IP address is used for in-game moderation, such as implementing IP bans.

C. External Services

We utilize external services, which have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies for further information:

PlayFab: Terms of Use Photon: Privacy and Cookie Policy

As we integrate more services, we will update this policy to include relevant information.

4. Data Deletion

To request the deletion of your data and the removal of your PlayFab account from our servers, please contact us.

5. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us.
